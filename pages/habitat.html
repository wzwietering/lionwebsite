The savana

Lions in the wild primarily live in savanas. These are wide plains with tall grass, which is eaten by there prey. The tall grass also helps the lion hide when it is sneaking up his prey.

Where in Africa?

Lions, can also live in other habitats that are like the savanas, because of this they can live on the entire african continent.


The red area is where you can find lions in the wild. It used to be much bigger, but human influence decreased it significantly.
